Introduction

Our company, Alglow, is a skin-care business specializing in beauty nourishment products, such as sunscreens, moisturizers, facial creams, etc. from laboratory-based marine algae. Our research shows that mycosporine-like amino acids (MAAs) are known for their natural compounds that block harmful ultraviolet radiation once utilized in skin-care. Therefore, enabling users to establish a healthier skin tone by protecting their skin from the sun, as well as providing their skins with hydration, nourishment and antioxidants.
